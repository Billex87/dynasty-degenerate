# Fantasy Football Product Workflow Audit – Dynasty Degens

This audit examines common fantasy football workflows to determine how well the app supports decision making.  Only the Skids Get Beat (dynasty superflex) context could be tested; workflows that depend on other league types remain unverified.

## Workflows evaluated

| Workflow | User goal | Current experience | League‑specific behaviour | Friction points | Missing context | Recommended improvements | Launch priority |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Entering a Sleeper username** | Provide login credentials and see list of leagues. | Landing page asks for Sleeper username and has a clear **Find Leagues** button. | Same for all leagues. | None observed. | Could use a label rather than placeholder. | Add a label and example (e.g., `Your Sleeper username`). | Low |

| **Switching between leagues** | Quickly jump to another league for comparison. | There is a bottom nav button labelled **Switch League** but it is non‑functional. | Should display same league list but does not. | Same as above. | Provide a functional dropdown or modal for switching. | Must fix |

| **Viewing player rankings** | See how players rank in dynasty or redraft context. | `Full Roster Rankings` table provides a comprehensive list; filterable by position and searchable. | Shows dynasty and season values and cross‑position comps. | The table is hidden behind a small bar; sort toggle is unclear; cannot compare across leagues. | Make table more visible by default; add column sorting; allow multi‑league comparison. | Should fix |

| **Viewing projections** | See projected points for upcoming week(s). | Momentum tab shows trending players but not explicit weekly projections. | **Sorting by stats** | Sort players by dynasty value, weekly rank or age. | Only a global toggle exists; no per‑column sorting. | Works but ambiguous. | Sorting control is subtle; cannot sort ascending/descending. | Add sortable column headers with arrows.  Provide sort direction and ability to sort by any numeric column. | Medium |

| **Viewing team/roster information** | Understand each manager’s team, strengths, weaknesses, positions. | Overview tab lists **Top Heavy**, **Thin Ice** and **Bench Rot** categories with manager avatars and roster values. | League-specific; good for dynasty. | Team pages are hidden behind links; cannot quickly see all team rosters. | Provide a `Teams` tab with roster, positions, bench depth and starting lineup suggestions. | Medium |

## Summary

The app offers many analytical tools but suffers from critical workflow blockers, particularly around league selection and cross‑league context.  Once the league switching bug is fixed, additional improvements are needed to support typical fantasy workflows, especially for redraft players who rely on weekly projections and start/sit guidance.  A streamlined mobile experience is also essential for real‑world usage.
