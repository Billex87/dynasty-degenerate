# Release Risk Register – Dynasty Degens

This register identifies potential risks that could impact the successful launch of Dynasty Degens.  Each risk is rated by probability (Low/Medium/High) and severity (Low/Medium/High), with notes on whether it is a launch blocker.  Mitigation steps and QA verification actions are provided.

| Risk ID | Risk | Area affected | League affected | User impact | Probability | Severity | Launch blocker | Mitigation | Codex implementation relevance | QA verification step |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **R‑001** | League switching overlay hangs, preventing access to other leagues. | Navigation & state management | All | Users cannot analyse multiple leagues; testers cannot verify cross‑league logic. | High | High | **Yes** | Fix localStorage persistence; ensure `Switch League` button works; add cancel/timeout to loading overlay. | Must implement stable league loader and state reset. | Attempt to switch leagues; confirm overlay finishes and other leagues load. |
| **R‑002** | TE premium and superflex adjustments are incorrect or absent. | Rankings, player modal, values | The Fantasy Degenerates, Skids Get Beat | Wrong player values and rankings could mislead users; QBs/TEs mis‑valued. | Medium | High | **Yes** | Implement scoring adjustments per league settings; display tags; adjust values and projections accordingly. | Ensure TE and QB values change when toggling TE premium and superflex. | Load TE premium and non‑premium leagues and compare TE/QB ranks. |
| **R‑003** | Dynasty metrics shown in redraft leagues. | Content & copy | Test League, Gov Tech Grid Iron | Redraft players may see dynasty values and long‑term context instead of current‑season focus. | Medium | High | **Yes** | Separate data sources for dynasty and redraft; show current‑season projections for redraft; hide dynasty language. | Adjust queries depending on league type. | Load redraft league and ensure no dynasty terminology appears. |
| **R‑004** | Draft‑incomplete leagues show roster‑dependent features. | Draft & roster analysis | Gov Tech Grid Iron | Pre‑draft leagues may incorrectly display roster analysis or trade grades. | Medium | Medium | Yes | Add pre‑draft checks; hide or disable features requiring rosters until draft completes; show empty states. | Condition components on `status` field. | Load draft‑incomplete league; verify pre‑draft empty states. |
| **R‑005** | Poor mobile experience due to dense tables. | Responsive design | All | Users on phones cannot use the app; high abandonment rate. | High | Medium | Yes | Implement responsive layouts (cards, sticky columns, condensed tables). | Use media queries; restructure components. | Test all major pages at 430px and 390px widths. |
| **R‑006** | Inaccessible UI: low contrast, missing labels, no focus states. | Accessibility | All | Users with disabilities cannot use the app; possible legal risks. | High | Medium | Yes | Improve contrast; add ARIA labels; implement keyboard focus; supply alt text. | Audit via automated and manual tools. | Use screen reader and keyboard to navigate; verify focus indicators. |
| **R‑007** | Comedic copy undermines trust or confuses users. | Microcopy & UX | All | Some users may think the app is a joke and not trust analytics. | Low | Medium | No | Tone down humour or combine with informative text; provide progress explanations. | Adjust copy and provide context. | Ask user testers if copy confuses them. |
| **R‑008** | API errors not surfaced to users; silent failures. | API integration | All | Users encounter blank screens with no explanation. | Medium | Medium | Yes | Implement error handling and display friendly error messages with retry options. | Add catch blocks and UI error states. | Simulate API failure; verify error messages appear. |
| **R‑009** | Unclear value basis (dynasty vs season vs weekly) in rankings. | Data presentation | All | Users misinterpret values; make poor decisions. | Medium | Medium | No | Label columns clearly (e.g., “Dynasty Value”, “Season Value”, “Weekly Rank”). | Add badges or column headers with units. | Inspect table headers; confirm clarity. |
| **R‑010** | Incomplete league settings display. | Settings & context | All | Users cannot see full roster/scoring settings; may misinterpret projections. | Low | Medium | No | Provide a dedicated league settings page summarising roster positions, scoring rules, TE premium multiplier and draft status. | Build settings component; fetch scoring settings. | Load league and access new settings page. |


This risk register should inform prioritisation of engineering and QA efforts.  R‑001, R‑002, R‑003, R‑004, R‑005 and R‑006 are considered launch blockers because they materially impact product correctness, usability and accessibility.  Other risks should be scheduled in the next iteration or addressed alongside major feature work.
