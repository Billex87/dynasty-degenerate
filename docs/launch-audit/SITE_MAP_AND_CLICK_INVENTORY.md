# Site Map and Click Inventory – Dynasty Degens

This document lists the discovered routes/pages, their purpose and major interactive elements.  Only pages accessible through the **Skids Get Beat** league could be inspected.  Attempts to switch to other leagues via the league list or `Switch League` button were blocked due to a progress‑overlay bug.

|---|---|---|---|---|

### Unreachable interactions

- **Tight end premium toggle** – Could not test because TE premium league could not be loaded.
- **Redraft‑specific features** – The redraft leagues (Test League, Gov Tech Grid Iron) could not be accessed, so features like draft completion states, start/sit guidance or non‑dynasty rankings remain unverified.
- **Empty/error states** – Since only a fully populated dynasty league was accessible, empty or error states (such as pre‑draft rosters) were not observed.
